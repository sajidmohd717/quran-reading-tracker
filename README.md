# Quran Reading Tracker

A simple, friction-free web app to track your Quran reading progress — no account, no install, just open and start logging.

## Live App

**[https://sajidmohd717.github.io/quran-reading-tracker](https://sajidmohd717.github.io/quran-reading-tracker)**

## Features

- Log reading sessions by page range (the Quran is 604 pages)
- See your current position and how far through you are
- Track your daily reading streak
- View your full session history with notes
- All data saved locally in your browser — no login needed

## How to use

1. Open the app at the link above
2. Tap **+ Log Reading Session**
3. Enter the pages you read (e.g. From Page 1, To Page 8)
4. Optionally add a note (e.g. "Surah Al-Baqarah")
5. Hit **Save Session** — your progress updates instantly

Your data is stored in your browser's local storage, so it persists between visits on the same device.
